Pricing

Simple, transparent pricing.

No monthly fees — ever. A one-time setup fee gets your site built and launched. After that, you pay just $25/yr for domain renewal — or nothing if you already own your domain. Our 3.5% platform fee is added at client checkout and paid by the guest, keeping your ongoing costs minimal and predictable. Stripe's standard payment processing fee of 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction is also passed to the guest and shown transparently at checkout.

Client Protections

You own your site.
Always.

✓
Your domain, your brand
Every platform lives on your own domain. Clients see your brand, not ours. We charge a flat $25/yr to cover renewal — and if you ever leave, the domain transfers to you, guaranteed.
✓
Payments route directly to you
We use Stripe Connect so client payments deposit directly into your Stripe account. Our 3.5% is split automatically — you never handle our portion.
✓
No surprise lock-ins
We ask for a 12-month initial term so we can deliver real results. After that, 30 days notice to leave — and your domain transfers to you, always.
✓
Chargeback protection built in
Every booking generates a confirmation email with your cancellation policy attached. A clear paper trail protects you and your clients through Stripe's built-in dispute tools.
✓
Built-in business reporting
Your admin dashboard includes revenue summaries, booking counts, cancellation rates, and top client reports — all pulled from your own data. No third-party tools, no extra fees.
No Hidden Fees. Ever.
Every charge that flows through your booking platform is clearly disclosed to your clients at checkout. You'll always know exactly what you're earning — and so will they.
