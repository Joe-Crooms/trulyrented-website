Our Revenue Model

Transparent by design.
Aligned with your success.

We make money when your guests book — not from you. Here's exactly how it works and what it looks like at scale.

Collected before work begins. Covers discovery, design, build, and launch. You pay once — we deliver a complete direct booking website.
📈

Client Protections

You own your site.
Always.

✓
Your domain, your brand
Every site lives on your own domain. Guests see your brand, not ours. We charge a flat $25/yr to cover renewal — and if you ever leave, the domain transfers to you, guaranteed.
✓
Payments route directly to you
We use Stripe Connect so guest payments deposit directly into your Stripe account. Our 3.5% is split automatically — you never handle our portion.
✓
No surprise lock-ins
We ask for a 12-month initial term so we can deliver real results. After that, 30 days notice to leave — and your domain transfers to you, always.
✓
Chargeback protection built in
Every booking generates a confirmation email with your cancellation policy attached. A clear paper trail wins most disputes through Stripe's built-in tools.
